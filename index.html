Let's create a wonder-filled journey together

I believe in building games with novel concepts, interesting mechanics, & satisfying gameplay that respect the players & the developers.

I've grown up loving games - ones that transport you to a different world, ones that make you laugh with friends, & ones that make you reflect on your own skills, life, & philosophies. Those are the games I strive to make.

Since 2001, I've been building games & growing my game developement skillset - primarily focusing on game design & programming. I firmly believe in making experiences that are meaningful and worthy of someone's time & money without tricking them or using psychology against them. I also believe in a work-to-live lifestyle, an inclusive work environment, & a no-crunch culture.

If you are nodding your head in agreement, let's work together.

Things I've Made

Each project I create begins with the intent to grow a specific skillset or to hone a specific game element. From testing art pipelines to experimenting with musical themes to collaborating with others, stepping outside of my comfort zone is a key requirement for every new concept.

In order to brave the waters of commerical products & consulting, I created my game development company, Echo Gameworks LLC, in 2019.

Reorbit

My first commercial game released in 2019, Reorbit is a minimalist puzzle game for mobile devices.

Game Goal: Match moons to the same color planets in as few moves as possible to beat each level. This is done by rotating the moons in clockwise patterns & then shooting them to other planets.

Development Intent: Gain experience working all the way through the release pipeline, publishing to app stores, using in-app purchases, & marketing a product. I created all the code, art, & music. My favorite aspect of Reorbit is the unique puzzle mechanics.

Reorbit was retired from app stores in 2021 & is no longer commercially available.

Hotdog Man's Delicious Intent: A Stealth Advertising Campaign for Ghost Pepper Frankfurters

Hotdog Man was created in one week with a team of two others for the Brackeys 2022.2 game jam. The theme was "You're Not Alone." The game earned 36th place overall (top 5%) & 7th in graphics out of 1,073 entries.

Game Goal: Sneak up on unsuspecting targets to plant an advertisement for Ghost Pepper Frankfurters in their pocket.

Development Intent: Learn how to work with others in a game development context. I led as the project manager, game designer, & programmer while collaborating with an artist (softcereal) & musican (SleepUFO). The artist had never worked on a game before, so there was also mentoring of game dev workflows & expectactions. My favorite part was the brainstorming with the other team members & being able to concentrate on a smaller subset of disciplines unlike what is required for solo game jams.

Play Hotdog Man on itch.io.

Nova Blossom

Nova Blossom was solo-created in one week for the Brackeys 2023.1 game jam with the theme "An End is a New Beginning." The game earned 32nd place overall (top 5%) & 7th in innovation out of 741 entries.

Game Goal: Cover the playing area with vines from the nova blossoms. In order to cover enough tiles, you must kill old nova blossoms to create new blossoms. Killing a blossom will make it go supernova, gaining one-time abilities for how much that blossom had grown.

Development Intent: To create strong mechanic paired with an interesting concept in as polished a package as possible within the time limit. I created all the code, art, & music. My favorite part of the game is the supernova effect when a blossom dies.

Play Nova Blossom on itch.io.

Prakzars Problematic Potion Puzzle Pit

Prakzar was solo-created in 48 hours for Ludum Dare 53 with the theme "Unstable." The game earned 144th place overall (top 5%) & 73rd in innovation out of 2939 entries.

Game Goal: Live for as long as possible in a pit of exploding potions while trying to get a high score.

Development Intent: Focus on developing my pixel art skills - specifically the character & potion animation - & to work more with the Unity physics engine. I created all the code, art, & music. My favorite part of the game was the character controller & the crazy interactions as the potions explode.

Play Prakzar on itch.io.

Arcane Cubes

Arcane Cubes was solo-created in 48 hours for the Game Makers Tool Kit 2022 game jam with the theme "Role of the Dice." The game earned 1,458th place overall (top 25%) out of 6,100 entries.

Game Goal: A take on the Tower Defense genre where you throw dice of different elements to fend off enemy waves. Each dice type has unique attacks which excel at destroying specific enemies.

Development Intent: To get more comfortable working in a 3D environment. All my previous projects had been 2D. I created all the code, art, & music. My favorite part of Arcane Cubes was working on the art direction with the modeled tower & dice versus the flat-standee type enemies.

Play Arcane Cubes on itch.io.
